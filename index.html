What is Irys?



Irys is a decentralized storage and scaling protocol.
It makes data permanent, verifiable and accessible,
helping Web3 apps scale without losing security or reliability.

Why Irys?

  • Permanent - data stays forever once uploaded.
  • Fast - optimized for speed and scalability.
  • Verifiable - every file can be proven authentic.
  • Cost-efficient - one-time payment, no recurring fees.

How it Works

  1. Upload your data through Irys.
  2. Data is stored permanently across the decentralized network.
  3. Anyone can access and verify it at any time.
  4. Developers can easily integrate Irys into apps and blockchains.

Use Cases

  • Storing NFTs and digital art.
  • Archiving DeFi transaction data.
  • Powering Web3 apps that need fast,
    permanent storage.
  • Hosting AI models, datasets, and
    research.

Community & Ecosystem

Irys is supported by a growing community of developers,
creators, and partners. From DeFi protocols to NFT platforms, more projects are choosing Irys for reliable, permanent storage. Join the movement and help shape the future of decentralized data.

The Future With Irys

Irys is more than storage - it's the foundation for the next generation of Web3 apps. By making data permanent, Irys is building the infrastructure for scalable blockchains, AI, abd decentralized innovation.
